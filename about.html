About the Music School

School of Music has a wide range of classes, suitable for all experience levels, from beginners to advanced and for all ages, starting from 5 years old! We are committed to developing a passion for music in all our students and to creating an environment where students can genuinely look forward to their lessons.

The Music School offers high quality Piano, Guitar, Drums, Violin, Trumpet and Singing lessons in North Dublin.
